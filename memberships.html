Membership

We welcome any hospital, health service, or organisation with an interest in Health Promotion to join the International HPH Network and become a part of a community with almost 600 health care institutions world-wide. Our network works to support all health care organisations in their efforts to be health-oriented and to ensure that prevention, treatment, and rehabilitation be viewed from a health perspective.

Member Benefits

Personalized Health Approach

There are evidence-based arguments for health care institutions to invest in health promotion and to increase focus on health promotion and health policy on health care. Patient and patient organizations will benefit from HPH because we focus on the perceived needs of patients and relatives, creating better chances for self-determination and self-care.

Quality Service & Care

These aim to achieve a better quality of hospital service and quality of care, while improving the sustainability of health outcomes. Our comprehensive approach ensures that every aspect of your health journey is supported by expert guidance and evidence-based practices.

Community & Support Network

Join a community of health-focused individuals and organizations dedicated to promoting wellness. Our network provides ongoing support, resources, and connections to help you achieve your health goals while contributing to a broader movement of health promotion.
