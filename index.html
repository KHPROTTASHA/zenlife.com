Frequently Asked Questions

What is functional or personalized medicine?+
Functional/personalized medicine focuses on identifying and addressing the root causes of health issues through comprehensive testing and tailored treatment plans. It's a patient-centered approach that considers your unique genetics, environment, and lifestyle.
Do you take insurance?+
We are primarily out-of-network but provide detailed superbills for you to submit to your insurance for potential reimbursement. We also accept HSA/FSA payments for eligible services.
What's included in the membership?+
Memberships typically include provider visits, health coaching, body composition scans, access to educational events, and discounts on additional services and retail products. Specific inclusions vary by membership tier.
Do you sell supplements?+
Yes, we offer a curated selection of high-quality supplements in-clinic and through our online dispensary, available exclusively to members. All supplements are third-party tested for purity and potency.
